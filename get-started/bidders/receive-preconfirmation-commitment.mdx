### Prerequisite
Make sure you get a [mev-commit Bidder Node up and running](https://docs.primev.xyz/get-started/quickstart)

### Submit a Bid and receive a Preconfirmation commitment

Change the `txHash`, `amount`, and `blockNumber` to your liking

```bash ❯_ terminal
curl -X POST <http://localhost:13523/v1/bidder/bid> \
-d '{
  "txHash": "91a89B633194c0D86C539A1A5B14DCCacfD47094",
  "amount": 20000,
  "blockNumber": 899099
}'
```

🎉 Congrats, you received your first L1 preconfirmation!
